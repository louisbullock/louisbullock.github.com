Ma

Website theme

Ma is a simple, clean theme for use with Scriptogr.am.

In the Japanese language, Ma roughly translates to 'gap', 'space', 'pause', or 'the space between two structural parts'.

Ma represents the negative space, between the positive space.

Thus, Ma is designed with negative space in mind, with a focus on whitespace, yet retains a balance with the positive space (the content).

Neat typography, built-in responsiveness, and an overall balanced aesthetic are cleverly integrated into Ma.

Ma was created for Scriptogr.am because I desired a theme that was ultra clean and minimal.

I drew inspiration from the principles I have been taught in my TAFE studies, and aimed to follow some, and break others.

I believe that this theme is an embodiment of my own design philosophy, my love of whitespace and clean design. It shows my love of the Japanese aesthetic, and hints at the Swiss aesthetic.
